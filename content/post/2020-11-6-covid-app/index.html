The Covid Tracker App allows one to visualize the number of cases and deaths by day on a State level. California, Hawaii and Florida are selected by defualt but you can add or remove States using the selector box in the sidebar. The accompanying maps use color to show the total cases and total deaths per million people by State. If you click on the map, there is an interactive tooltip that provides you with detailed information by State.
